Site Name:

The Weather Watch

Site Purpose:

With being a community site that provides weather information the focus is unity and edification. Theres a handful of features that help enforce these core ideas like for instance theres a live news feed section where users can post live updates of weather conditions and news related to the weather. Post follow an up-vote/down-vote system to weed out irrelevant information. Natural disasters can be tragic for certain families and individuals. Things like floods, fires, and blizzards can physically harm community members or destroy their possessions. To bring the community together in support theres a space in the app where some on can go to learn about how they can give back. Either by donating money to a specific cause or joining in community service this app will help direct you to the most current and relevant options. Also with being a site that provides weather information its important that this information is exposed to the client in an easily consumable fashion. Graphs and charts are a good way to communicate weather conditions by time. Its understood that life is demanding and The Weather Watch makes it easy to plan around one of the few things we don't have control over which is the weather.
