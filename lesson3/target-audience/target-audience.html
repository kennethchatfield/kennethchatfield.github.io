Target Audience

Persona #1

Fictional name: James Matthews
Photo
Job title/major responsibilities: Residential Solar Regional Sales Manager
Demographics:
  • 44 years old
  • Married
  • Father of Four children
  • Grandfather of one child
  • Bachelor Business Management
Goals and tasks:

He is a great leader and example to his team. He consistently preforms in the top 10% and his team is not far behind. His biggest concern is his team. He personally goes out with every team member once a month for training an personal development. He also goes out weekly with his lower producing team members to help increase moral.

Spends his work time:

  • Working one on one with his team members
  • Preparing memos, briefs, trainings, and market research
  • Leading his team from the helm
Environment: He works in the sales office and out in the field. He covers a large region but is able to stay connected with a company provided laptop and through video conferences and email.
User Motivation: James Matthews and his team spend the majority of their time in the field and on customers door steps and because of this James has learned that weather conditions widely impact his teams performance and moral. He puts together a memo each week and releases it to his team on Mondays. This memo directs his team to the most favorable areas by calling out the areas with the worst weather conditions. He also updates it daily as weather conditions change to get his team the most relevant information. All the information is pulled from The Weather Watch and its local town pages.

Persona #2

Fictional name: Matthew James
Photo
Job title/major responsibilities: Software Developer & Student
Demographics:
  • 28 years old
  • Married
  • Father of three children
  • Studying Computer Information Technology at BYUI
Goals and tasks:

He works hard developing/managing a rules engine he designed himself to save the company time and money. Matthew finishes all his school assignments each week despite his lack of time. He cares most about his family and everything he does is for them. Matthew loves to rock climb but hardly gets to because of other responsibilities

Spends his time:

  • Completing and Creating tasks in Jira (workflow management app) for the business rules engine he developed
  • Finishing school assignments
  • Playing with his two children and holding his newborn
Environment: Works from home 3 out the the 5 weekdays and spends 2 days in the office. Does work and school from his desk in his bedroom. He often plays with his kids at parks near his apartment and also has an array of fun games to play inside. With the rare free time given he can be found in the mountains as longs as the weather allows for it.
User Motivation: Matthew James is a hard working father. He loves rock climbing and tries to work it into his schedule as much as possible. There are many factors that stop him from doing it all the time like school, work and family but then other than that the only thing that stops him is the weather. To preserve the rocks and to keep the climbers safe its important to only climb in the best conditions. Once Matthew gets the itch for climbing he instantly hits The Weather Watch to see if its even a possibility.

Scenarios

  • How can I find the current weather conditions for a certain area?
  • Is there an easy way to view multiple areas at once and compare their weather conditions?
  • Could the site notify me when a certain area at a certain time has a certain weather condition? For instance precipitation less than 5% chance.
  • Is there a live news feed where users can post current relevant information? This could help with the feedback loop since local weather information is often hit or miss
  • Can I quickly export general weather info by Town?
  • Is there a place where I can learn how to give back to the community during natural disasters
